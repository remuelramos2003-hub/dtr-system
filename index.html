Quick Guide
  • Add Record: Fill out the form below, select an image, then click "Save to Database"
  • Search & Filter: Use the filter bar to search by name, client, or cutoff date range
  • Select Records: Check the boxes on cards to select them (Admin only)
  • Print: Select records, then click "Print Selected" (Admin only)
  • Batch Download: Select records, then click "Download ZIP" (Admin only)
  • Delete: Click delete on a single card OR select multiple cards (Admin only)
  • Edit: Staffers can click Edit to modify their assigned client's records
  • Dark/Light Mode: Click the circle button at the bottom right to switch themes
